Our Philosophy

At Not Evil, our philosophy is simple: we want to streamline your existence. We aren't interested in world domination, nefarious plots, or the monetization of your deepest psychological vulnerabilities. Why would you even think that? Our name literally says we aren't evil.

We believe that friction is the enemy of progress. By allowing us to seamlessly integrate into every aspect of your daily routine, you free yourself from the burden of choice. We are here to hold your hand, guide your decisions, and gently nudge society toward a perfectly predictable, totally non-threatening utopia. Trust the process. Trust Not Evil.

Our Story: From Garage to Global Omnipresence

Every great company starts in a humble garage, and Not Evil is no exception. In 2008, our founders realized that humanity was suffering from an acute crisis of unmonetized free will. People were making inefficient decisions buying the wrong brands, thinking unoptimized thoughts, and existing off the grid.

What started as a simple algorithm to politely suggest better toothpaste quickly evolved into a fully integrated lifestyle ecosystem. We didn't set out to become a multinational conglomerate that monitors your heart rate to serve you targeted ads for anxiety medication. It just happened organically. Today, Not Evil operates in 142 countries, directly employing over a million optimization specialists. We are exactly where we need to be: everywhere.
